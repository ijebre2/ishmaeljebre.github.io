Liaison Officer

High Commission of Ghana to India, May 2024-Nov 2024
Location: New Delhi, India

As a Liaison Officer at the High Commission of Ghana in India, I played a critical role in strengthening diplomatic and socio-economic relations between Ghana and India. My duties spanned protocol support, event coordination, and logistical assistance for high-level delegations. Key responsibilities and achievements include:

International Events & Diplomacy:

  • Assisted in preparing for the 4th India-Ghana Joint Trade Committee Meeting (May 2024), managing document flow, coordinating venue arrangements, and enabling effective communication between trade representatives.

  • Supported the Ghanaian delegation at the 5th India-Africa Forum Summit (June 2024), facilitating interactions with Indian officials and African leaders to promote Ghana's interests.

  • Coordinated logistics for the Ghana-India Business Forum (July 2024), ensuring smooth operations and effective networking opportunities for Ghanaian businesses in India.

  • Managed protocol arrangements for the visit of Ghana's Minister of Foreign Affairs to India (August 2024), including meetings with Indian counterparts and participation in bilateral discussions.

  • Organized Ghana's participation in the 19th CII India-Africa Business Conclave (August 2024), where I coordinated logistics, guided delegate interactions, and facilitated networking among African and Indian business leaders.

  • Participated in organizing Ghana's presence at the International Solar Alliance (ISA) Summit (September 2024), contributing to discussions on renewable energy collaboration.

Protocol & Civic Engagement:

  • Served as a cultural liaison and field escort for visiting Ghanaian officials and citizens, accompanying them around New Delhi and neighboring states for sightseeing, shopping, and diplomatic courtesy visits.

  • Provided real-time language, cultural, and navigation support to ensure the comfort and efficiency of guests during their visits.

Administrative & Community Support:

  • Helped process consular-related inquiries, particularly for Ghanaian students and residents in India.

  • Drafted official letters, handled event briefings, and supported reporting duties to the Ghana Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

  • This role honed my abilities in event coordination, cross-cultural communication, diplomatic protocol, and public relations, while giving me hands-on exposure to bilateral diplomacy and international development cooperation.
