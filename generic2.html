Teacher Intern & Mathematics Instructor

Sahrich Academy, Ghana

  • Teacher: May 2017 - Aug 2017

  • Intern: May 2019 - July 2019


As both a full-time teacher and returning intern at Sahrich Academy, I was responsible for delivering core STEM instruction to basic and junior high school students. My work focused on simplifying difficult concepts and creating interactive classroom experiences to boost student engagement and performance in mathematics and science.

Key responsibilities:

  • Taught mathematics and integrated science to students at various levels, aligning lesson plans with Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum standards.

  • Developed simplified notes, visual aids, and practical demonstrations to explain abstract topics like algebra, geometry, chemical processes, and human biology.

  • Provided personalized academic support to struggling students, helping them improve test scores and build confidence.

  • Organized mock exams and led revision classes ahead of the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).

  • Assisted with basic school administration tasks, student assessments, and teacher-parent feedback sessions during the internship period.

These roles strengthened my classroom leadership skills, creativity in teaching, and commitment to inclusive, student-centered education.
